## Derivative of a constant

$$
\begin{gathered}
\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\frac{c-c}{x_1-x_0}=0
\\\\
f'(x)=0
\end{gathered}
$$

> For $y=f(x)=c$

## Derivative of a line

$$
\begin{gathered}
\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\frac{a(x+\Delta x)+b-(ax+b)}{\Delta x}=a
\\\\
f'(x)=a
\end{gathered}
$$

> For $y=f(x)=ax+b$
